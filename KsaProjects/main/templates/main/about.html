About the Vision

Learn the strategic goals behind the Kingdom’s tourism projects: sustainable growth, cultural celebration, and community development.

The Vision Realization Programs (VRPs) are the driving force behind Vision 2030, working to bring the Vision to life. Each VRP has a specific roadmap with approved delivery plans, focused on meeting specific objectives and key performance indicators within five years. As Vision 2030 evolves to meet the changing needs of Saudi Arabia, the VRPs adapt and align with these needs to ensure Vision 2030 is successfully achieved.
