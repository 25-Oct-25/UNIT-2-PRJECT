Alula

Where History, Nature, and Imagination Unite.

AlUla is a living museum of ancient civilizations and timeless beauty.

Home to boundless cultural treasures, including Saudi Arabia’s first UNESCO World Heritage site, AlUla is transforming into a once-in-a-lifetime experience for visitors from around the world.

With over 200,000 years of largely underexplored history, including the Nabataean city of Hegra, the ancient Kingdoms Dadanite and Lihyanite, and AlUla Old Town, AlUla’s rich heritage and bright future are open to explore.

The Royal Commission for AlUla is committed to a sustainable and responsible approach to the area’s urban, economic, and heritage development. Initiatives across archaeology, tourism, culture, education, and the arts are helping open this extraordinary destination to the world, all while preserving the area's remarkable natural and historic character.

Explore how Vision 2030 is transforming the Kingdom into a global destination for history, heritage, culture, and nature.
