Deseret Rock

Carved by Nature, Designed for Luxury.

It combines sustainability, architecture, and raw beauty in perfect harmony.

Announced in 2021 by Red Sea Global, Desert Rock is a tribute to sustainable tourism and a perfect blend of modern luxury and environmental preservation. The resort has been crafted to allow guests to connect with the natural beauty of the region while protecting it for future generations.

Featuring 50 villas and 10 hotel rooms nestled among the magnificent vistas, the architecture is fully integrated into the rock, preserving its stunning silhouette and paying homage to the Oppenheim Architectural philosophy of building with the land, not on it. Desert Rock will preserve the rich history of the site, with local community members serving as stewards of the land and providing educational tours for visitors.

The resort will achieve the highest Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) certification level, reducing energy consumption and regenerating native flora. Water retention and distribution systems will be used throughout the site, and materials excavated will be used to create the infrastructure.
