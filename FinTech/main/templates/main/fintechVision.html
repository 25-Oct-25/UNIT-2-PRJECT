FinTech: The Engine of Vision 2030

FinTech is not just a sector; it's the core engine for achieving the ambitious goals of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030.

payments

A Cashless Society

The primary goal is to increase non-cash transactions to 70% by 2030. This is driven by e-wallets and digital payments, reducing reliance on physical cash.

hub

A Global FinTech Hub

To position Riyadh as a top 10 global FinTech hub. Initiatives like 'Fintech Saudi' and the SAMA 'Regulatory Sandbox' are built to attract and grow startups.

rocket_launch

Economic Diversification

Moving the economy beyond oil by empowering Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs) with new digital financing, lending, and banking solutions.
