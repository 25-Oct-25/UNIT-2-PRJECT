Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is a nation undergoing a massive transformation, moving rapidly from its historical identity as a conservative kingdom primarily defined by its role as the birthplace of Islam and a global leader in the oil industry. For decades, its strength lay in its vast energy reserves and its spiritual significance. Today, guided by the bold Vision 2030 framework, Saudi Arabia has embarked on an ambitious journey to diversify its economy, empower its youth, and reform its society, resulting in remarkable achievements like the flourishing of arts, entertainment, and non-oil sectors through broad social changes and widespread infrastructural development. Looking forward, the Kingdom is focused on pioneering the future through giga-projects like NEOM, becoming a global hub for technology and finance, and driving significant international engagement in sports and tourism, solidifying its place as a dynamic and influential player on the world stage.
