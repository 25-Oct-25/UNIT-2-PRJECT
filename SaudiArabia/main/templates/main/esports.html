E-Sports Saudi Achievements

eWorld Cup

Mosaad Al-Dossary

Mosaad "Msdossary" Al-Dossary is widely recognized as one of the most accomplished players in competitive EA Sports FC (formerly FIFA), with his career defined by several major titles, most notably winning the FIFA eWorld Cup Grand Final in 2018 where he secured a dominant 4-0 aggregate victory. Beyond that career-defining win, the Saudi Arabian player has achieved over 20 trophies, including multiple FUT Champions Cups and the Xbox World Championship in 2019, making him the only person in the game's history to win back-to-back Xbox World Championships. His consistent performance and dominance led to him being named Esports Player of the Year twice. Furthermore, he is the founder and chairman of the successful esports organization Team Falcons, demonstrating his impact both as an individual competitor and an influential figure in the growth of esports in Saudi Arabia.
