Sports Saudi Achievements

Defeating Argentina

FIFA World Cup 2022

With unparalleled national fervor and stunning determination, the Saudi National Football Team delivered one of the most iconic and historic upsets in the entire history of the FIFA World Cup on November 22, 2022, securing a glorious 2-1 victory over the favored Argentina. Despite conceding an early goal to the legendary Lionel Messi, the Green Falcons—driven by coach Hervé Renard's tactical brilliance and a refusal to yield—launched an unforgettable second-half surge. The nation erupted with joy following the lightning-fast equalizer from Saleh Al-Shehri and the truly sensational, winning strike from Salem Al-Dawsari. This victory did more than just break Argentina's 36-game unbeaten streak; it instantly became a defining moment of national pride and triumph on the world stage, rightly celebrated by a nationwide public holiday.

Silver Medal

Tareg Hamedi

Tareg Hamedi cemented his place as a national hero by seizing a spectacular Silver medal for Saudi Arabia in the highly anticipated debut of Men's +75 kg Kumite Karate at the Tokyo 2020 Games. Displaying unyielding courage and skill, Hamedi powered through the competition to reach the final. In a dramatic gold medal bout, he executed a stunning, decisive kick while leading 4-1, showcasing the Kingdom's prowess. Although he was controversially disqualified for using excessive force, denying him the gold, his incredible journey and masterful performance were met with immense national pride and celebration, forever marking his achievement as a pivotal moment in Saudi Olympic history.
