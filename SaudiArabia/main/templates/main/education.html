Educational Saudi Achievements

Saudi students, supported primarily by the King Abdulaziz and His Companions Foundation for Giftedness and Creativity (Mawhiba) and the Ministry of Education, have achieved remarkable success on the global stage, particularly in STEM fields.

ISEF

International Science Fairs

Saudi students have established a strong presence at major global innovation events, most notably the International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF). Represented by the Mawhiba program, the Kingdom's young researchers consistently achieve high award tallies each year, frequently securing more than 25 Grand and Special Awards in diverse fields like energy, chemistry, and biomedical engineering. This continuous success has resulted in a substantial cumulative record of over 185 international awards in ISEF alone since 2007, affirming their aptitude for original scientific research and innovation.

Olympiads

International Olympiads

The Kingdom's students demonstrate academic excellence across a broad range of scientific disciplines by winning numerous medals in the International Scientific Olympiads. These competitions span fields such as Mathematics (IMO), Chemistry (IChO), Physics (IPhO), and Biology (IBO). Through consistent preparation and participation, Saudi teams have collectively garnered an impressive cumulative total of over 795 international awards across all Olympiads, including Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals, cementing Saudi Arabia's position as a top-performing nation among Arab countries in rigorous academic contests.
