About NEOM

NEOM is a region in the making in northwest Saudi Arabia, conceived to support the diversification of the Kingdom’s economy and deliver urban solutions so that people and planet can thrive in harmony. Powered by renewable energy and driven by innovation - NEOM is redefining business, livability and conservation.

NEOM Regions & Landmarks

SINDALAH

NEOM's premier island destination in the Red Sea, offering a luxury yacht club, world-class beaches and vibrant marine life. It sets a new standard for exclusive tourism.

TROJENA

The regional mountain hub of NEOM, redefining year-round outdoor adventure and alpine tourism. It will feature ski slopes, a freshwater lake and premier wellness resorts.

OXAGON

The world's largest floating industrial complex, driving advanced and clean industries in NEOM. It aims to redefine the future of sustainable manufacturing and innovation in the region.
